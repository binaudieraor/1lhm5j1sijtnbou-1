<p><ul><li>Ventricular Assisted Device (VAD)</li><li>Total Artificial Heart (TAH)</li><li>Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The circulatory support devices market comprises several key types. Ventricular Assisted Devices (VADs) assist heart chambers in pumping blood, used in heart failure management. The Total Artificial Heart (TAH) replaces heart function entirely for severely affected patients. Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) provides prolonged cardiac and respiratory support by oxygenating blood outside the body, serving critically ill patients. Each device plays a pivotal role in enhancing patient outcomes in various stages of cardiac dysfunction and failure.</p></p>
