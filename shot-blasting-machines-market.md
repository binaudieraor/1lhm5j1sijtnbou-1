<p><p>The shot blasting machines market is diverse, featuring several types: </p><p>1. **Hanger Type**: Ideal for large or heavy parts, hanging on hooks allows for thorough cleaning and surface preparation.</p><p>2. **Tumblast Machine**: Uses a tumbling barrel to process small to medium-sized parts, efficient for batch operations.</p><p>3. **Continuous Through-feed**: Designed for high-volume production, it allows parts to move continuously through the blasting area.</p><p>4. **Rotary Table**: Provides efficient processing for medium-sized components, with the table rotating for uniform blasting.</p><p>5. **Others**: This category includes specialized machines tailored for unique applications and industries.</p></p>
